About Dignity Art Edge

A marketplace built so independent artisans keep both the credit and the majority of every sale.

Our Mission

Dignity, by design.

Dignity Art Edge started in 2014 in a single rented stall at a Jaipur craft fair, where our founders watched master artisans sell work worth ten times more once it carried a "curated" label they didn't control. We built this studio to put that control back in the maker's hands.

Today every listing carries the artisan's name, region and process — not as a marketing flourish, but because we believe a piece is worth more when you know exactly whose hands made it.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Fair Partnership

Artisans set their own prices and keep the majority of every sale, with no exclusivity required.

Real Provenance

Every catalog placard is verified against the maker's own studio records before a piece goes live.

Slow, Considered Craft

We favour studios that work in small batches over factories chasing volume.
